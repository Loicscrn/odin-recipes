Omelet

Difficulty Level ✭✭✭✩✩

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 tablespoon butter or oil

Recipe

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl
  2. Whisk the eggs with a fork until the yolk and whites are fully combined
  3. Add a pinch of salt and pepper to the mixture. Stir to combine
  4. Heat the pan. Place a non-stick skillet over medium heat and add the butter or oil. Let it melt and coat the bottom of the pan.
  5. Pour the eggs.
  6. Cook the omelet. Let the eggs cook undisturbed for about 1-2 minutes until they start to set around the edges.
  7. Gently push the edges toward the center with a spatula, allowing the uncooked eggs to flow to the edges.
  8. Fold and serve
